Hey! I'm Bee Wertheimer (they/she), a narrative designer and game developer based in Brooklyn, NY. I belong to the inaugural BFA Game Design class at Pratt Institute, alongside a Minor in Creative Writing. My work explores what it means to love and hurt—to be loved and hurt—and is always informed by my personal experiences. I tell stories I wish I had heard when I was (more) young and scared, in the hopes that others will feel recognized and take comfort in them.
