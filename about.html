—about

I'm Bee Wertheimer (they/she), a writer, narrative designer, and game developer based in Brooklyn, NY. I belong to the inaugural BFA Game Arts class at Pratt Institute, alongside a Minor in Creative Writing. My work explores what it means to love and hurt—to be loved and hurt—and is always informed by my personal experiences. I tell stories in the hopes that others will feel recognized in them.
