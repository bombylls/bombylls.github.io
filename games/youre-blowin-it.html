you're blowin' it!

art direction, animation, ui/ux


Work together to keep the bubble afloat with your breath!

This couch co-op arcade game was made in 48 hours with Drew Shapiro in Godot for Global Game Jam 2025.

art direction

The jam's theme was "bubble," and I immediately thought of children playing in the bath. All assets, both diegetic and UI, were drawn digitally with crayon-textured brushes and hand-animated for an imperfect, human touch.
